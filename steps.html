How to Get the Answers

Step 1

In the homepage, click on "Get Started".

Step 2

Upon reaching the next page, you'll encounter a list of classes. Choose your class here.

Step 3

Now, a list of books from the selected class will be presented. Select the books from which you need answers.

Step 4

In the search bar, enter your question and then press "Enter".
